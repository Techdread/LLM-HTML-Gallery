This simulation visualizes how massive objects curve spacetime according to Einstein's theory of general relativity. Particles follow geodesics (straight paths in curved spacetime) which appear as curved trajectories to observers.

The grid deformation represents the curvature of spacetime, with greater mass creating deeper gravity wells.
